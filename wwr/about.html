WATERMARK RAFTING COMPANY

Our purpose at Watermark Rafting is to connect people with the power and beauty of rivers, offering thrilling rafting experiences that transcend the ordinary. We strive to build a community of adventure enthusiasts, fostering a love for water sports and instilling a sense of responsibility towards the preservation of our natural resources. By combining expert guidance, state-of-the-art equipment, and a commitment to environmental sustainability, we aim to be a catalyst for transformative outdoor experiences that inspire a lifelong connection to the world's waterways

History

Founded in 1998 by river enthusiasts, Watermark Rafting Company blends excitement, safety, and a deep love for nature. Starting with a single raft, we've grown into a symbol of premier adventure, offering diverse excursions from thrilling rapids to serene floats. Our commitment to safety, skilled guides, and upgraded equipment define us. With multiple locations on iconic rivers, we share the joy of exploration globally. We're a mark of adventure and environmental stewardship, dedicated to preserving the beauty of waterways. As we innovate for the future, we foster a deep connection between people and the rivers that shape our memories.
